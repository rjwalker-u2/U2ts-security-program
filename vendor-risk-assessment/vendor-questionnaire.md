1. Organization & Governance

1.1 Do you have an Information Security Program based on SOC 2, ISO 27001, or NIST CSF?

1.3 Do you conduct annual risk assessments?

3.1 Is data encrypted at rest (AES-256 or equivalent)?

4. Vulnerability & Patch Management

4.1 How often do you perform vulnerability scanning?

4.3 Do you use automated patching tools?
4.4 Do you conduct third-party penetration testing annually?
4.5 Do you provide results or attestation of remediation?

5.2 What is your typical incident response timeline?
5.3 How do you notify customers of security incidents?
5.4 Do you have a 24/7 monitoring capability?
