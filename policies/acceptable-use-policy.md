Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

Company: U2 Technology Solutions (U2TS)
Policy Owner: Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)
Approval Date: [Insert date]
Version: 1.0

1. Purpose

The purpose of this Acceptable Use Policy is to define appropriate, responsible, and secure use of U2TS technology resources, including laptops, mobile devices, networks, applications, cloud services, and customer data.

2. Scope

This policy applies to:

All employees

Contractors

Interns

Third-party service providers

Any individual accessing U2TS systems, networks, or data

3. Acceptable Use Requirements

All users must:

Use U2TS systems for authorized business purposes only

Protect company data from unauthorized access

Immediately report security incidents to security@u2ts.com

Follow strong password practices

Lock their workstation when unattended

Use only approved software and tools

Store company data only on approved locations (OneDrive, SharePoint, etc.)

4. Prohibited Activities

Users may NOT:

Install unauthorized software

Disable security tools (EDR, AV, logging, VPN)

Attempt to bypass access controls

Access, modify, or delete data without authorization

Use company resources to engage in illegal activity

Transfer company data to personal devices

Share credentials or MFA tokens

Use personal email accounts to conduct company business

5. Monitoring & Privacy

U2TS reserves the right to:

Monitor activity on company systems

Log and review network traffic

Audit access to sensitive systems

Users should not expect privacy on company-owned devices or networks.

6. Enforcement

Violations may result in:

Revocation of system access

Disciplinary action up to termination

Legal action if applicable

7. Review Cycle

This policy will be reviewed annually or when significant changes occur in the business or threat landscape.
